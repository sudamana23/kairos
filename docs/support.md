## Frequently Asked Questions

**Where is my data stored?**
Everything is stored locally on your Mac. There is no cloud, no account, and no server. Your data lives in your Mac's Application Support folder and never leaves your device.

**How do I back up my data?**
Go to Settings → Data Management → Export Backup. This saves a JSON file to a location you choose. Store it in iCloud Drive, an external drive, or anywhere you like. To restore, use Import Backup from the same section.

**How do I move my data to a new Mac?**
Export a backup on your old Mac, transfer the file to your new Mac (AirDrop, iCloud Drive, or USB), then open Tenets on the new Mac and use Import Backup.

**Why isn't Apple Intelligence working?**
Apple Intelligence requires macOS 26 or later and a compatible Apple Silicon device. If your device does not meet these requirements, Tenets falls back to manual entry throughout — all features remain available.

**The app shows "Kairos" in Notification permissions in System Settings.**
This is a macOS caching issue from an earlier version of the app. Go to System Settings → Notifications, find the old entry and disable it. Tenets will then request permission fresh under the correct name.

**How do I reset the app and start over?**
Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → Reset All Data. This permanently deletes everything and returns the app to its initial state.

**Can I use Tenets on iPhone or iPad?**
Tenets is currently macOS only. It is designed for deliberate, focused planning — the kind of work that suits a larger screen and keyboard.
