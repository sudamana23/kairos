# Tenets

**Annual planning. Life-first.**

Most planning tools optimise for output. Tenets starts with your values — and works backwards to what your year should actually look like.

---

## What it is

Tenets is a private, on-device life planning tool for people who want to live deliberately — not just productively.

It starts with your values: the three to five timeless principles your life is actually built around. From there, you set domains — the areas of life you'll invest in this year. Then objectives. Then key results. A weekly pulse keeps you honest. A monthly review keeps you aligned.

Everything is processed on your Mac. Nothing leaves your device.

---

## How it works

**Values first.**
Before you plan a single goal, Tenets asks what your life stands for. Apple Intelligence can guide you through three reflective questions and suggest values based on your answers. Or write them yourself.

**Annual OKRs for your life.**
Domains replace teams. Objectives stay ambitious. Key results stay honest. The framework that runs great organisations, applied to the one project that actually matters.

**A weekly pulse.**
Two minutes. Energy level, a theme, a short note. The compound effect of staying honest with yourself, week by week.

**A monthly review.**
Rate your key results. Have a short structured conversation about what moved and what didn't. On-device AI summarises your answers into a clear written record.

**A long view.**
Time Machine shows year-over-year trends, monthly activity, and key result history — the shape of the years, not just the last 30 days.
